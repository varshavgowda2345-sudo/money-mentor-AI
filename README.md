# WealthPath AI — AI-Powered Financial Freedom Assistant

> **Plan Smarter. Retire Earlier.**

WealthPath AI is a modern AI-powered fintech application designed for ordinary people who do not have strong financial knowledge. The platform acts as a smart financial mentor that helps users understand money, manage expenses, reduce debt, plan investments, and achieve financial independence earlier in life.

The app focuses on:

* Simplicity
* Accessibility
* Privacy
* AI-driven personalization
* Beginner-friendly financial guidance

---

#  Problem Statement

Millions of people earn money but struggle with:

* budgeting
* debt management
* understanding investments
* insurance confusion
* financial planning
* retirement preparation

Existing finance apps are often too complex and built for financially educated users.

**WealthPath AI simplifies finance using conversational AI and smart automation.**

##  5. Smart SMS-Based Expense Tracking

(Optional & Privacy-Focused)

The app can analyze financial SMS alerts like:

* UPI payments
* Salary credits

### Important Security Rules

The system NEVER accesses:

* Passwords
* Authentication codes
* Verification messages

### AI Features

* Subscription detection
